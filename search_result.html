Breaking Bad

  • Rating: ★★★★
  • Release date: January 20th, 2008
  • Seasons: 5
  • Género: Drama

Breaking Bad is an American drama television series created and produced by Vince Gilligan. It tells the story of Walter White a financially strapped chemistry teacher who is diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer. To pay for his treatment and secure his family's financial future, he begins cooking and selling methamphetamine, along with Jesse Pinkman, a former student of his. The series, set and produced in Albuquerque (New Mexico), is characterized by its desert settings and the tendency in history to put its characters in situations that apparently have no way out, which led its creator to describe it as a contemporary western.
